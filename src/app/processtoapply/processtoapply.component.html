Simple Steps to Your Dream Home

Our streamlined application process makes getting a home loan quick and hassle-free

1

Check Eligibility

Use our online eligibility calculator to see how much you can borrow based on your income, expenses, and credit score.

2

Submit Application

Fill our simple online application form with your personal, financial, and property details. It takes less than 10 minutes.

3

Document Submission

Upload required documents including ID proof, address proof, income documents, and property papers through our secure portal.

Documents Needed:

  • KYC Documents (PAN, Aadhaar)
  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Property documents
  • Bank statements (6 months)
4

Verification & Approval

Our team will verify your details and documents. You'll receive an in-principle approval within 48 hours of document submission.

5

Loan Disbursement

After property valuation and final approval, funds will be disbursed directly to the builder/seller's account as per agreement.
