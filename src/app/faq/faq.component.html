Loan - Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who can apply for a loan?

Any salaried or self-employed individual meeting the minimum age (21 years) and income criteria can apply.

2. What types of loans do you offer?

We offer Personal Loans, Home Loans, Business Loans, Education Loans, and Vehicle Loans.

3. What is the loan tenure available?

Loan tenure typically ranges from 12 to 60 months for personal loans and up to 30 years for home loans.

4. What interest rates do you offer?

Interest rates are competitive and based on your credit profile. They typically start from 10.5% p.a. onwards.

5. What are the required documents?

Standard documents include PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, recent salary slips or IT returns, and bank statements.

6. How long does approval and disbursal take?

7. Can I prepay or foreclose the loan early?

Yes, most loans can be prepaid or foreclosed after a minimum lock-in period. Charges may apply based on the loan type.

8. What if I miss an EMI?

A missed EMI may result in penalties and impact your credit score. We recommend setting up auto-debit for timely payments.

9. Is a guarantor required?

Most personal and home loans do not require a guarantor unless your profile does not meet our eligibility criteria.

10. How can I check my loan status?

You can track your application and EMI status anytime through our online portal or mobile app.
