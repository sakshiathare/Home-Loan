The basic eligibility criteria include:

  • Minimum age: 21 years at loan commencement
  • Maximum age: 65 years at loan maturity
  • Minimum income: ₹25,000 per month (varies by city)
  • Minimum work experience: 2 years for salaried individuals
  • CIBIL score: 650 or above

You'll need to submit:

Identity & Address Proof:

  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport
  • Voter ID

Income Proof:

  • Last 3 months salary slips
  • Form 16 or ITR for last 2 years
  • 6 months bank statements

Property Documents:

  • Sale Agreement
  • Title Deeds
  • NOC from builder (if applicable)

Our current interest rates range from 8.40% to 10.15% p.a. depending on:

  • Loan amount and tenure
  • Applicant's credit score
  • Type of employment (salaried/self-employed)
  • Special offers for women borrowers (additional 0.05% discount)

Use our EMI Calculator to estimate your payments.

Yes, you can prepay your home loan with the following conditions:

  • No prepayment charges for floating rate loans
  • Fixed rate loans: 2% prepayment charge
  • Minimum prepayment amount: ₹25,000 or 1 EMI, whichever is higher
  • Maximum 5 prepayments allowed per financial year

Prepayments can significantly reduce your total interest burden.

Our typical approval timeline:

  • 24 hours: Initial eligibility check
  • 3-5 days: Document verification and processing
  • 2-3 days: Property valuation
  • 1 day: Final approval and sanction letter

Complete digital processing can reduce this to 72 hours for simple cases.

The loan amount depends on multiple factors:

  • Up to 90% of property value for loans below ₹30 lakh
  • Up to 80% for loans between ₹30-75 lakh
  • Up to 75% for loans above ₹75 lakh
  • Maximum loan amount: ₹10 crore
  • EMI/NMI ratio not exceeding 50%
